      <p>The <b>Dilleniales</b> are an <a href="/wiki/Order_(biology)" title="Order (biology)">order</a> of <a href="/wiki/Flowering_plants" class="mw-redirect" title="Flowering plants">flowering plants</a>, potentially containing one family, <a href="/wiki/Dilleniaceae" title="Dilleniaceae">Dilleniaceae</a>. The <a href="/wiki/APG_III_system" title="APG III system">APG III system</a> of 2009, like the earlier <a href="/wiki/APG_II_system" title="APG II system">APG II system</a> of 2003, left the Dilleniaceae unplaced as to order, while noting that the name Dilleniales was available.<sup id="cite_ref-APGIII2009_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-APGIII2009-1">[1]</a></sup> Stevens at the <a href="/wiki/Angiosperm_Phylogeny_Website" title="Angiosperm Phylogeny Website">Angiosperm Phylogeny Website</a> has subsequently placed Dilleniaceae in the order Dilleniales.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> </p>
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/Cronquist_system" title="Cronquist system">Cronquist system</a>, of 1981, recognized such an order and placed it in subclass <a href="/wiki/Dilleniidae" title="Dilleniidae">Dilleniidae</a>. It used the following <a href="/wiki/Circumscription_(taxonomy)" title="Circumscription (taxonomy)">circumscription</a>:<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> </p> 
      <ul>
       <li>order Dilleniales

         <dd>

          family 
          <a href="/wiki/Paeoniaceae" class="mw-redirect" title="Paeoniaceae">Paeoniaceae</a>
         </dd>
        </dl></li>
      </ul> 
      <p>The <a href="/wiki/Takhtajan_system" title="Takhtajan system">Takhtajan system</a> of 1969 had the families Dilleniaceae and <a href="/wiki/Crossosomataceae" title="Crossosomataceae">Crossosomataceae</a> in the order.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> </p>
      <p>The APG III system assigned the second of these families, the Paeoniaceae, to the order <a href="/wiki/Saxifragales" title="Saxifragales">Saxifragales</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-APGIII2009_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-APGIII2009-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
