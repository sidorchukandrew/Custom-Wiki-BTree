   <h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">1971 Kensington and Chelsea London Borough Council election</h1> 
   <div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">

    <div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr">
     <div class="mw-parser-output">
      <p> The <b>1971 Kensington and Chelsea Council election</b> took place on 13 May 1971 to elect members of <a href="/wiki/Kensington_and_Chelsea_London_Borough_Council" title="Kensington and Chelsea London Borough Council">Kensington and Chelsea London Borough Council</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, England. The whole council was up for election and the <a href="/wiki/Conservative_Party_(UK)" title="Conservative Party (UK)">Conservative party</a> stayed in overall control of the council.<sup id="cite_ref-London_1971_election_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-London_1971_election-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
