      <p><i><b>Mammillaria backebergiana</b></i> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Cactus" title="Cactus">cactus</a> in the subfamily <a href="/wiki/Cactoideae" title="Cactoideae">Cactoideae</a>. It is native to <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a> and can be found on cliffs at elevations of around 1,900 m.<sup id="cite_ref-iucn_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-iucn-1">[1]</a></sup> </p>
