      <p><b>Claudio Mezzadri</b> (born 10 June 1965 in <a href="/wiki/Locarno" title="Locarno">Locarno</a>) is a retired professional <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a> player from <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>. Mezzadri turned professional in 1983, and won his only <a href="/wiki/ATP_Tour" title="ATP Tour">ATP</a> title four years later. He also won four doubles titles in his career. </p>
      <p>Mezzadri participated in 9 <a href="/wiki/Davis_Cup" title="Davis Cup">Davis Cup</a> ties for <a href="/wiki/Switzerland_Davis_Cup_team" title="Switzerland Davis Cup team">Switzerland</a> from 1987 to 1991, posting an 11–4 record in singles and a 3–2 record in doubles. </p>
